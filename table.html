Explanation of Dynamic Effects:

  • Click On Row: When you click on a table row, it is highlighted in red to indicate that it has been selected.
  • Hover Over Row: When you hover over a row, it changes the background color to light blue. If the row is selected, it will show yellow.
  • Mouse Away From Row: When you move your mouse out of a row, it will reset the background color unless the row is selected.
  • Mouse Double Click Row: Deletes Row
